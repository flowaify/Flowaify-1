Buyer and seller inquiries go cold when agents are busy or on site. Flowaify replies instantly, routes to the right agent, syncs the CRM, and follows up automatically — so no inquiry slips through.

Home Services

Home Service Businesses

Quote requests and missed calls come in while you're on the job. By the time you check your phone, the lead has moved on. Flowaify responds the moment a form is submitted or a call is missed.

Med Spas & Clinics

Med Spas & Clinics

Consultation inquiries arrive after hours through Instagram, forms, and referrals. Front desks can't keep up. Flowaify handles every inquiry 24/7, collects intake information, and confirms appointments automatically.

More consultations booked, fewer no-shows, without extra front desk hours.
Local Services

Local Service Businesses

High-intent leads from Google, Facebook, and directories arrive scattered across platforms with no single system to catch them. Flowaify captures every source, responds instantly, and keeps the CRM clean.
